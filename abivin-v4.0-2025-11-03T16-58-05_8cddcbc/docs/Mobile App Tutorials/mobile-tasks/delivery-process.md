To complete the delivery process at stores, deliverymen have to proceed these tasks below:\
Step 1: Click the [Delivery] task detail

![395](https://files.readme.io/e2ba8a9-2019-01-22_17-19-30.jpg "2019-01-22_17-19-30.jpg")

Step 2: Take check-in photo at stores

![394](https://files.readme.io/b736579-2019-01-22_17-17-54.jpg "2019-01-22_17-17-54.jpg")

Step 3: Choose delivery status

![395](https://files.readme.io/4f287c3-2019-01-22_17-20-27.jpg "2019-01-22_17-20-27.jpg")

* If the order is successfully delivered, the status will be chosen as ‘Successfully Delivered’, then enter the collected cash

![397](https://files.readme.io/be0e0e2-2019-01-22_17-21-28.jpg "2019-01-22_17-21-28.jpg")

* If the order is partially shipped, the status is chosen as ‘Partly Delivered’. Update the actual amount of delivered goods and received cash
* If the order is unable to deliver, the status to choose is ‘Failed To Deliver’. The system will require deliverymen to choose one reason from the list that makes the order fail to deliver\

**Note:**\
