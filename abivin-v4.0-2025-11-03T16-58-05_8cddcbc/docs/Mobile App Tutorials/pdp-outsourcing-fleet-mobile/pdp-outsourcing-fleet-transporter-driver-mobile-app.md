* You are the driver of the Transporter. Below we will guide you on how to perform your tasks on the Mobile App

## Sign in

* On the **Tasks** tab will show the list of all delivery routes you need to perform
* Each route consists of several tasks: ***Start at the Transporter garage; Pick up products at the origin Depot; Deliver products at the destination Depot*** and ***Come back to the Transporter garage, end the working day***
* You can tap on the task title to move to the task screen, where you need to perform several actions and submit the task

<Image title="task list.png" alt={422} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/0efe75a-task_list.png" />

* You need to perform the tasks in the correct sequence displayed on the app screen
* If the tasks are in a different date, you can tap on the icons :fa-chevron-left: and :fa-chevron-right: to go to the appropriate date. You can also tap directly on the date and choose the appropriate date from the pop out calendar

<Image title="task list.png" alt={422} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/af8e504-task_list.png" />

<Image title="task list.png" alt={844} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/21a65fd-task_list.png" />

## 1. Perform "Start at Transporter garage" task

* In this task, you start at the Transporter garage
* To get to the task detail screen, tap on the text **Start** on the task list screen

## View order information

* You can tap on the icon :fa-caret-up: of the **Order Information** bar to view the information of the order you need to deliver

<Image title="order info.png" alt={840} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/002ad82-order_info.png" />

## Check in at Transporter garage

* You must first perform **Check-In** task, proving that you have come to the Transporter garage, by taking a Check In picture
* Tap on the camera icon :fa-camera:, then take picture with your mobile camera

<Image title="check in photo.png" alt={413} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/ce68f7b-check_in_photo.png" />

## Submit task

* After you have taken the Check In picture, tap on the text **Submit** at the top right of the screen

<Image title="order info.png" alt={422} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/7239bec-order_info.png" />

## 2. Perform "Pick up products" task at the Origin Depot

* In this task, you will travel to the Origin Depot to pick up products
* To get to the task detail screen, tap on the the name of the Origin Depot on the task list screen
* Before traveling to the Origin Depot, you can call the Depot personnel to ensure they are opening by tapping on the icon :fa-phone: on the **Phone number** row
* You can tap on the icon :fa-share-square: on the **Address row** to view the direction from your current location to the Origin Depot

<Image title="customerphone1.png" alt={415} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/266d62a-customerphone1.png" />

## Check in at the Origin Depot

* Upon arriving at the Origin Depot, you need to Check-In by taking photo 
* [The instruction is similar as when you check in at the Transporter garage](https://docs.abivin.com/docs/pdp-outsourcing-fleet-transporter-driver-mobile-app#section-check-in-at-transporter-garage)

## 3. Perform "Deliver products" task at the Destination Depot

* In this task, you will travel to the Destination Depot, deliver products to the warehouseman and select the delivery result
* To get to the task detail screen, tap on the the name of the Destination Depot on the task list screen
* Before traveling to the Destination Depot, you can call the Depot personnel to ensure they are opening by tapping on the icon :fa-phone: on the **Phone number** row
* You can tap on the icon :fa-share-square: on the **Address row** to view the direction from your current location to the Destination Depot

## Check in at the Origin Depot

* Upon arriving at the Destination Depot, you need to Check-In by taking photo 
* [The instruction is similar as when you check in at the Transporter garage](https://docs.abivin.com/docs/pdp-outsourcing-fleet-transporter-driver-mobile-app#section-check-in-at-transporter-garage)

## Select delivery result

* To select the delivery result of an order, tap on the order code. It will display the order information as well as the delivery result section

<Image title="order result.png" alt={425} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/4078a6f-order_result.png" />

* There are four possible results for the delivery status of an order: 
* **Completed**: The order is completed, all products have been delivered
* **Partly Delivered**: Some, not all products have been delivered
* **Redundant**: More products have been delivered than originally stated in the order
* **Not Completed**: None of the products have been delivered
* Each result is represented by a radio box. To select a result, tap on the corresponding radio box

<Image title="order result.png" alt={425} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/cb9f20e-order_result.png" />

* Aside, if the products exceed the capacity of the vehicle, you can tap on **Overload** radio box

<Image title="order result.png" alt={425} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/0823078-order_result.png" />

* If the result is **Not Completed**, you also need to specify the reason, by tapping on the text **Select Reason** below the **Not Completed** option. You will be directed to a screen, listing the reasons. Tap on the appropriate reason to select it

<Image title="order result.png" alt={884} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/034cda5-order_result.png" />

* If there are multiple reasons, you can tap on the search icon :fa-search:, input a part of the reason you want to find into the search bar in order to filter faster

<Image title="find reason.png" alt={422} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/a609f90-find_reason.png" />

* Finally, you can tap on **Submit** on the top right of the screen to submit this task

## 4. Come back to the Transporter garage, perform "End of Day" task

* In this task, you come back to the Depot after completing the deliveries to return undelivered products as well as perform other necessary procedures
* Tap on the text **End** on the task list screen to get to the task detail screen

## Check In at Transporter garage

* You need to Check In upon your come back to the Transporter garage
* [The instruction is similar as when you check in at the Transporter garage](https://docs.abivin.com/docs/pdp-outsourcing-fleet-transporter-driver-mobile-app#section-check-in-at-transporter-garage)
