## Issue 1: Not able to perform any task

## Description

* You are not able to perform tasks on Mobile app, such as submitting task, checking in etc.

## Reason

* It is probably because of weak or non-existent Phone/Internet signal at your location

<Image title="2019-09-03 16_28_31-Window.png" alt={575} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/a5e2c9e-2019-09-03_16_28_31-Window.png" />

## Solution

* You should move to a location with strong Phone/Internet signal
* You may have to turn off your Wifi/3G/4G mode then turn on again
* You may even need to restart your Mobile phone

## Issue 2: Check-in task hangs at "Loading..." status even with strong Internet signal

## Description

* The **Check in** task hangs indefinitely at the **Loading...** status after you have taken photo, even though you are standing at a location with strong Internet signal 

<Image title="i2uVEVVuA2UtN2s9WLSbC-TpsxuUkna9og.png" alt={770} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/360bdeb-i2uVEVVuA2UtN2s9WLSbC-TpsxuUkna9og.png" />

## Reason

* This could be due to your mobile phone running out of memory, which prevents the app from saving your photo

## Solution

* Try removing some unused apps, moving old photos/videos from your mobile phone to your computer to clear up the memory space

## Issue 3: Not able to submit tasks after a specific time

## Description

* You are not able to submit tasks after a specific time point

## Reason

* This is probably due to the time requirement set up by your managers

## Solution

* You have to submit tasks within the required time period defined by your manager
