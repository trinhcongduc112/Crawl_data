* This function is built to help manage the Bill of Materials\
  **(This only applied for some users)** 
* To be able to manage the information of the products by Serial Number, the user needs to set up their Product List properly.

## Create Product

* Locate **Inventory** tab from **Products > Inventory** tab
* Click on **Create** to create a new Product

![1920](https://files.readme.io/218d49b-1.png "1.png")

* With the Parent Product, on the **Product Details** tab, Input suitable information into the information field and choose Tracking **By Serial No** on the **Serial Numbers** tab.
* On the other hand, with Lower Level product, choose **No Tracking** while creating this type of Product

![1920](https://files.readme.io/5f6e132-2.png "2.png")

![1920](https://files.readme.io/3ef0e04-3.png "3.png")

* Also, products can be assigned to their related **Parent Product** for the specific purpose or type of products that require managing materials of the products
* The **Parent Product** field can be found at the Create Product form with a variety of choices related to the product created

![1920](https://files.readme.io/9912b16-1.png "1.png")

* Click **Save** to finalize the product creation process

## Import Product

* Click on **Import** button to open the Upload Data table

![1920](https://files.readme.io/9b3b189-1.png "1.png")

* The sample excel file can be acquired by clicking on **Download Sample** then fill in the Excel file with suitable information for each field
* On the Upload Data table, select **By Serial Number** and upload the excel file
* In the case of a duplicate serial number, the system will have a notification to show the error and the specific reason for the error

![1920](https://files.readme.io/9f93727-2.png "2.png")

## Products Management

* Locate tab Products  >  Choose Inventory tab and click on the Product that is Tracked by Serial Number. Then choose the Serial Number tab to check the status of the Product
* The Product Status will be updated automatically after a task related to that product code is marked done on the mobile app
* The Product status can be updated manually on the Web through the Serial Number field 
* There are three statuses  (Stored, Sold and Paused) correspond to the tasks on Mobile App which will be based on the work with customers

![1920](https://files.readme.io/8cd9843-3.png "3.png")
