## Step 1: Enable the Reports tab

* Navigate to **Organizations > Group List**
* On the account that you want to grant the rights to use reports, click on **Edit** :fa-pencil: icon
* On the **Group Information** window, scroll down to **Modules** table.
* On **Reports** row, tick on boxes for the rights you want to assign to this user account.
* Click **Save** to commit the changes.
* Now, the **Reports** tab will appear on the left side panel.

## Step 2: Export reports

* Navigate to **Reports > Report**
* Click on **Report Type** field, choose the report you want to export from the drop down menu.
* Click on **Date Range** field, choose the starting date and the ending date to create the date range you want to export, then click **Apply**
* Finally, click on **Download** :fa-download: to download the report to your computer. Depends on the size of data and the connection status at the time of exporting, it might take a while for Abivin vRoute server to process.
