## Notifications

A notification is a message that is displayed in the status bar of the device to notify the user that the application has completed a task.\
Step 1: To enable notification, click on your Profile icon and choose Edit profile. Then choose “receive notification” and save your profile.

![1348](https://files.readme.io/1eb3b2a-H4.png "H4.png")

![1347](https://files.readme.io/aa780e8-H5.png "H5.png")

Step 2: Click 'Message' icon, then select View all to view all notifications

![1347](https://files.readme.io/d50bd8a-H3.png "H3.png")

After selecting View all, a list of notifications will appear in the Settings / Notifications tab as below picture: 

![1346](https://files.readme.io/87379a9-H6.png "H6.png")

List of notifications:

* Account **name\_of\_account** created order with code **order\_code**
* Account **name\_of\_account** optimized route for the day **date**
* Account **name\_of\_account** locked route for the day **date**
* **name\_of\_account** checked-in task **name\_of\_task**at the wrong location over 200m
* **name\_of\_account** checked-out task **name\_of\_task**longer than the specified time
* **name\_of\_account** checked-in late at the depot, task **name\_of\_task**
* **name\_of\_account** checked-in task **name\_of\_task**out of the specified time

## Geo-fencing

Notification when the delivery men check in out of the 200m zone, and check in at the store for more than 15 minutes

1. When the delivery men check in out of the 200 meters zone, the icon will be colored red on the map.
2. When the delivery men check in for more than 15 minutes, the icon will be colored red on the map.

![1920](https://files.readme.io/6e4ccad-H8.png "H8.png")

The supervisor can see which delivery men are checking in out of the 200m zone or check in at the store more than 15 minutes.
