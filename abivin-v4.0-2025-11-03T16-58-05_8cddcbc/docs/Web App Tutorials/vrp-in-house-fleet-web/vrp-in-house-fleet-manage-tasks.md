* Right after you have locked the optimized Delivery Routes during the Route Plan optimization process, the delivery tasks of the Route Plan will be generated and will show up on the Mobile app of the vehicles' drivers as well as the task list on the Web app

## Locate Task List

* The delivery tasks are listed on **Tasks > Tasks** tab

- By default, the delivery tasks of the Delivery Routes generated on the current day will show up on this tab
- As the assigned vehicles' drivers perform the delivery tasks on the Mobile app and submit the results onto the Web app, you can refresh this tab to update the task results

## Manually Update Task Result

* If some drivers, due to some reasons, can not submit the delivery task results on their Mobile app, then you can manually update their delivery task result
* To do that, follow the steps below:
* Step 1: Click on **Edit** icon :fa-pencil: of the delivery task

## Filter Tasks

* To search the delivery tasks of Delivery Routes generated on a specific date or during a date range (Both past and future dates), you can use the filter function
* To do that, follow the steps below:
* Step 1: Click on the funnel icon above the top right corner of the task list table. The task filter form will appear

- Step 2: On the task filter form, click on the field parallel to the funnel icon. A drop-down list will appear. This drop-down list shows the attributes of the tasks

* Step 3: On the drop-down list, click on the **Start Date** attribute. After that, a date field will appear to the right of the form

- Step 4: Click on the date field. A calendar form will pop out. Select the appropriate date (or date range). The selected date (or date range) here equals the Route Plan date during the Route Plan optimization process. Click **Apply** on the calendar form to confirm the selected date or date range
