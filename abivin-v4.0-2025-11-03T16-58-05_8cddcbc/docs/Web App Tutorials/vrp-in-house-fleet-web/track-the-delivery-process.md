In the delivery process, deliverymen will check in, check out at warehouse/store.

1. To check the progress, just hover over the location on the navigation bar of a deliveryman to see check in, check out photos at the warehouse/shop.
2. If the check in to check out duration is short, the image will be displayed in a bar on the timeline (without the marker icon)
3. The icon of driver shows the current location of the deliveryman
4. The icon of marker show the original coordinates location of the shop\
   All stores must be checked in before updating order status and check out before you can check in again at the next store
