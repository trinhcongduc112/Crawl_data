* To export ***Promotion Audit by Store*** and ***Promotion Audit by Customer*** reports, please follow the steps below
* Navigate to **Reports** tab
* Click on **Select Action Report** field
* Select the desired report from the drop down menu

<Image title="2019-10-11 12_06_20-Window.png" alt={1675} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/da9dd8d-2019-10-11_12_06_20-Window.png" />

* Click on **Select Month** field, choose the suitable month from the drop down calendar

<Image title="2019-10-11 14_06_55-Window.png" alt={1523} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/619dd72-2019-10-11_14_06_55-Window.png" />

* Click on **Select Organization** field, choose the suitable Organization from the drop down menu

<Image title="015.png" alt={1676} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/2034219-015.png" />

* Click on **Post Mail** field, choose the suitable Post Mail IDs from the drop down menu

<Image title="013.png" alt={1676} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/9ebda76-013.png" />

* Click on **Promotion Name** field, choose the suitable Promotion campaign from the drop down menu

<Image title="017.png" alt={1676} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/f5a1cfa-017.png" />

* You can also type the Promotion campaign name in the search bar to filter quicker

<Image title="2019-10-11 12_01_56-Window.png" alt={656} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/1e9e02e-2019-10-11_12_01_56-Window.png" />

* Click on **Store List** field, choose the suitable store from the drop down menu

<Image title="012.png" alt={1676} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/c96e143-012.png" />

* Click on **Export** button to download the report with the parameters recently configured

<Image title="005.png" alt={1676} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/c0efbc5-005.png" />

* The report in Excel template will be generated automatically and can be downloaded after a few moments
* **Note**: For the **Post Mail, Promotion Name** and **Store List** fields, you can either choose to select one or several values by clicking directly on the corresponding values

<Image title="2019-10-11 11_55_50-Window.png" alt={387} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/5de5bf1-2019-10-11_11_55_50-Window.png" />

* or click on **--All--** to select all values

<Image title="2019-10-11 11_56_21-Window.png" alt={388} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/ddb761d-2019-10-11_11_56_21-Window.png" />
