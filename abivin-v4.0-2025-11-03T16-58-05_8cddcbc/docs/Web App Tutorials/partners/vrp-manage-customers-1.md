## Search and filter customer

## Search customer

* To search for a specific customer, type either their *Title*, *Customer Code*, *Customer Name*, *Email* or *Mobile Number* in the search :fa-search: field

## Filter customer by depot/xDock

* You can filter customers who receive deliveries from a specific Depot/xDock by clicking on **Organization** :fa-caret-down: field, type the *Organization name* of the Depot/xDock into the field. Customers who receive orders from that depot/xDock will show up shortly

## Filter customer by Customer group or Province/City

* You can filter customer who belong to the same Customer group, or locate in the same Province/City by clicking on the corresponding column, then select the Customer group or Province/City from the drop down menu. Customers who meet the selected conditions will show up shortly

> üëç You can combine several filters for more accurate search results
