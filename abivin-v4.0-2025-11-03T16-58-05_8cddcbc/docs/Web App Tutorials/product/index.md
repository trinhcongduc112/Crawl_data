Products and Services are reasons for businesses to exist. Business owners hire people to produce goods or services. Goods can be a computer, a table, a car or shampoo bottle. Services can be lawyer service, janitor service or delivery services - where the intangible exchange of value occurs.

With all of those definitions, businesses want to index a range of products that they produce. When product variants increase in number over time, they are grouped into Product Categories. The purpose of Product Categories are two-folded: internally to help businesses easily manage the strategies of sales, marketing or production for each category with similar characteristics; externally to help customers or consumers to understand what is being offered, they compare the price of products to talk high or low about it, competitors identify products in the same categories to develop strategies to encounter.
