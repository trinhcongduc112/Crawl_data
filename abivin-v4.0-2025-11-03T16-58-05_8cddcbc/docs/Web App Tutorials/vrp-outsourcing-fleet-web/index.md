* VRP - Outsourcing Fleet Model is a VRP Model in which the delivery fleet is not under direct control of your organization's Depots. Instead, they belong to the Transporters - the organizations with which you sign transport service contracts. The Transporter delivery fleet will travel from their parking garage to your Depots, pick up products and deliver to your customers
