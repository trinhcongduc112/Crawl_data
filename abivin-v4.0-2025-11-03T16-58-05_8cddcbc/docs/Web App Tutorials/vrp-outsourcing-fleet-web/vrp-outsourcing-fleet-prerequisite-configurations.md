* Below are the compulsory configurations of this model

## Enable Outsourcing fleet model

* Navigate to **Organizations > Organization List** tab
* Click on **Edit** icon :fa-pencil: of the ***Manufacturer***
* Click on **Outsourcing** radio boxes under **Transporter** and **Warehouse** fields
* Click **Save** to confirm the change

## Enable Transport service

* Navigate to **Organizations > Organization List** tab
* Click on **Edit** icon :fa-pencil: of the ***Manufacturer***
* Click on the field below the text **Transport Service By**, then select **Product** from the drop down menu
* Click **Save** to confirm the change
