## Locate report tab

* Navigate to **Reports > Report** tab
* In this tab, you will be able to export reports

## Container by Shipments report

* To export this report, follow the steps below:
* Step 1: Click on the field below the text **Report Type**. Select **Container by Shipments** from the drop down menu
* Step 2: Click on **Date Range** field. Select the appropriate date range from the drop down calendar, then click **Apply** to confirm the date range
* Step 3: Click on **Select Shipment** field. The drop down menu will list out shipments that have been created within the selected date range. Select the shipment which you want to export
* Step 4: Click on **Download**. The system will gather data according to the criteria, then will export the report as a downloadable Excel template
