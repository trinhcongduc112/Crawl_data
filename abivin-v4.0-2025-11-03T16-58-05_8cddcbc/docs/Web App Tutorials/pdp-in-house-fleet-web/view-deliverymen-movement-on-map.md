When deliveryman moves for delivery, the system will update their current position 

1. To track the route of a deliveryman, click on that deliveryman ID and click the [Replay] button 
2. The timeline (red line) will show the progress of the deliveryman in real time.
3. To pause the playback, click [Pause]. To continue, click [Continue] 
4. To stop watching, click [Stop]
