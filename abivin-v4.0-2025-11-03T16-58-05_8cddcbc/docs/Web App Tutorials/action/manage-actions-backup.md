## Integrate Forms into Actions using Form Builder tool

* An Action after being created is just like an empty container, having nothing inside. You would have to integrate a
* In Abivin vRoute, we have equipped a very powerful Form builder tool, enabling our clients to quickly create just about any web form they want to cover all the physical tasks of the drivers or sales staffs just on one Mobile screen
* Below are the basic steps to integrate a Form into an existing action:
* Click on **Form Builder** :fa-cog: icon under the **Edit** column of that Action
* The **Form Builder** screen will appear
* On the left side of the screen there will be a panel consisted of three sections: **Basic Components**, **Special Components** and **Layout Components**
* Clicking on each section will reveal the various components that you can create
* To create a specific component, click on that component then drag and drop that component onto the right side of the screen
* The setup screen of that component will automatically appear
* There are many configurations that you can make with each component. Among them, there are some basic configurations that are quite fundamental to every form:
* Under the **Display** tab, you can create the label - the *name* of the form when it displays on Mobile, by inputting in the **Label** field
