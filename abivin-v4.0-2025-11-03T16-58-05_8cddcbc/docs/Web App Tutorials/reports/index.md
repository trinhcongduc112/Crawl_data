Reports in Abivin vRoute are highly flexible. We provide task-based operational reports, single-purposed analytical reports and custom reports of somewhere in between.

Operational reports are grouped by Actions and contain the detailed information completed in each Tasks. The piece of information can be a Number, Text or a Link to the complex components such as a photo, a picture of customer's signature or an Geo-address. The reports are useful for direct mid-managers who want to understand operation, root-cause, discover 

Analytical reports or analytical dashboards are grouped into Resources defined in Organizations. Each report contains a number of real-time and single-purposed questions. Each question either answers a number like total number of customers or a 2-dimensional of trends or group-by function. The report's real-timeness can be in the range of 1, 5, 10 and so minutes. The reports are viewable on a tablet, a monitor screen and acting as the highlight of business/organization health for top management team.

Custom reports are reports customized for specific needs. These has been used by at least a customer or you can request a new one.

Operational Reports answers "Who bought X?" while Analytical Reports answers "How many people bought X?". Depending on the level of management, a Manager or an Employee want to read a certain type of reports.

## Operational Reports

An Operational Report contains the "raw data" of answers in submitted tasks.

## Analytical Reports

An Analytical Report contains the aggreegated data of answers. In general, it answers the sum, count or average of a certain quantity. Understanding of data can be deduced from statistical metrics like this, therefore there is an increasing demand for these kinds of reports.

In order to produce effective Analytical Reports, vRoute often presents with a 2D vertical barchart. Vertical barchart is the most effective charts among others like pie chart, horizontal barchart or area chart. This should resolves 70% of the needs for data visualization. Having said that, user is able to change from barchart to any other available chart format from our Business Intelligence tool.
