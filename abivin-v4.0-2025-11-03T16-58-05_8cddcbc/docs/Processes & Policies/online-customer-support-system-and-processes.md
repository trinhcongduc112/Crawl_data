For online support, customers can choose one of the following methods:

## I. Abivin Helpdesk

Click on this link: [https://abivin.freshdesk.com/support/home](https://abivin.freshdesk.com/support/home)\
Step 1: Log into Abivin Helpdesk.\
There are 2 ways to Login:

Option 1: Log in via your Facebook or Google account.

![960](https://files.readme.io/70c451f-Untitled_presentation_10.jpg "Untitled presentation (10).jpg")

Option 2: If you do not have a Facebook or Google account, click the sign up icon on the right of the screen. Complete the form and click register.

![960](https://files.readme.io/b2e246d-Untitled_presentation_12.jpg "Untitled presentation (12).jpg")

After successful registration, you can log in the system by clicking the login button on the right of the screen. Complete the form and press login.

![960](https://files.readme.io/a6ef14c-Untitled_presentation_13.jpg "Untitled presentation (13).jpg")

Step 2: After successfully logging in, click on the support Ticket icon to create a ticket.

![960](https://files.readme.io/fd0efc3-Untitled_presentation_14.jpg "Untitled presentation (14).jpg")

Step 3: Enter all information with (\*)

![960](https://files.readme.io/9213689-Untitled_presentation_15.jpg "Untitled presentation (15).jpg")

Step 3.1: Enter your email address in the email section.

Step 3.2: Enter the title of your issue in the title section.\
Example: Title: "Can't optimize the route"

Step 3.3: Specify description of the problem in the description section.\
A detailed description will help us to figure out what the problem is easier.

Note: You should attach the Screenshot of the problem or error.

Step 4: After filling in all information, click submit.

![960](https://files.readme.io/7f843f4-Untitled_presentation_19.jpg "Untitled presentation (19).jpg")

Step 5: The system will display Ticket after it has been successfully created.\
Customer support department will receive and respond as soon as possible.

![960](https://files.readme.io/ccf123b-Untitled_presentation_20.jpg "Untitled presentation (20).jpg")

## II. Abivin Chat Support

Step 1: Log in to Abivin vRoute account and go to the chat icon on the bottom left corner of the screen as follows:

![2518](https://files.readme.io/78d6399-3.png "3.png")

Step 2: Click on the icon and on the screen will display the following contents:

![2432](https://files.readme.io/a294b20-6.png "6.png")

Step 3: Click language: English or Vietnamese

![2504](https://files.readme.io/a3a6d81-4.png "4.png")

Step 4: Describe the content of the problem then press send to send the message.

![2524](https://files.readme.io/58202c0-5.png "5.png")

## III. Call Abivin Help Center

Call the number (+84) 2462 767 159, inform switchboard operator with the details of the incident notice during the working time from 8:30 to 18:00.\
Customer support department will receive questions and confirm answers.
