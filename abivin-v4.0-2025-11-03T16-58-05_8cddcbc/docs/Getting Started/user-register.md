* This section will provide extensive guides on how to register an account on Abivin vRoute, including:
* Register a new account
* Register a Payment
* First-time Login
* Manage Payment Method
* Manage Subscriptions

## Register a new account

* To begin the registration process, please sign-up for a new account by visit at [https://www.abivin.com/pricing](https://www.abivin.com/pricing)

* We provide 4 different pricing plans suitable for various organizations' needs.

* You should choose the plan which has enough features and is suitable for your organization's operation. Then click on the "Start Free Trial" button

* You will be redirected to the checkout page. We provide 2 sections here: Your information and the payment method.

* Next, you need to enter your email in the Email\* field

<Image title="Screenshot from 2022-11-23 08-58-08.png" alt={939} src="https://files.readme.io/8d8107c-Screenshot_from_2022-11-23_08-58-08.png">
  Customer Info
</Image>

* Then, click on the Continue button.
* After our site administrator has verified your email, we will send to you 2 emails:
* * An "Welcome Email" with instructions. You should follow our instructions after login into our system for the first time
* * An "Create a new Password Email". You can ignore this email.

## Register a Payment

* After registering your email, a Payment section will appear
* We provide international payments, like VISA or MasterCard. 

<Image title="Screenshot from 2022-11-23 09-07-24.png" alt={574} src="https://files.readme.io/0171a38-Screenshot_from_2022-11-23_09-07-24.png">
  Payment
</Image>

* You should provide full payment information. This information will be strictly protected. 
* Then, click on Buy Now button
* A confirmation email will be sent to your registered email, with the details of your registration, including Plan Price, Plan Start, and Plan End. Besides, we provide the Username and Password used to login into our system.

<Image title="Screenshot from 2022-11-23 09-19-57.png" alt={899} src="https://files.readme.io/180419b-Screenshot_from_2022-11-23_09-19-57.png">

</Image>

## First-time Login

* Check your email and open the confirmation email, you will see the Username and Password
* Click on START EXPLORING button, you will be redirected to login screen  [https://vaccount.abivin.com/sign-in](https://vaccount.abivin.com/sign-in)

<Image title="Screenshot from 2022-11-23 09-33-10.png" alt={839} src="https://files.readme.io/81dc36c-Screenshot_from_2022-11-23_09-33-10.png">
  Login
</Image>

* Enter the Username and Password in the corresponding field. Then, click on the SIGN IN button. You should be entered our system. 
* We provide Onboard Tours, which guide you step-by-step from the first login to the moment you have a first route plan
