## Why I cannot plan the route when there is no order available?

**Case**: Customers see the notice showing that ***"There is no order available"***.

![509](https://files.readme.io/dd27d40-2222222.png "2222222.png")

**Reason**: There is no order available on the date of the route plan.

<Image title="9efc351d-493f-48ea-b439-2283ad01c9ac_large.png" alt={1600} width="smart" src="https://files.readme.io/0f7fbfc-9efc351d-493f-48ea-b439-2283ad01c9ac_large.png" />

**Resolution**: Input the orders information to plan the route. You can learn how to do it [here](https://docs.abivin.com/docs/vrp-outsourcing-fleet-manage-sales-orders).
