## Follow these instruction below to change password on the Website:

**Step 1. Click on the drop-down arrow next to your account name at the top right-hand side of your screen and select "Change Password".** 

![1920](https://files.readme.io/da5ecfc-passchange1.png "passchange1.png")

**Step 2. Fill in all required fields including Current Password, New Password, and Verify Password**. 

![1920](https://files.readme.io/3b91c33-passchange2.png "passchange2.png")

> ðŸ“˜ Notice
>
> * When filling in New Password field, please follow the system suggested conditions to create a strong password.
> * When verifying your new password, please type in exactly the same password just filled in above. Otherwise, the system will not allow to change the password.

**Step 3. Click on "Change Password".** 

![1920](https://files.readme.io/468a07a-passchange3.png "passchange3.png")

* After finishing the last step, the system will pop-up a notification of "Password Changed successfully".

![1920](https://files.readme.io/d7bda7b-passchange4.png "passchange4.png")
