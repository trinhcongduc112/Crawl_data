## Follow these instruction below to change password on the Website:

Step 1. Click on the drop-down arrow next to your account name at the top right-hand side of your screen and select "Change Password".

![1920](https://files.readme.io/df28c7e-da5ecfc-passchange1.png "da5ecfc-passchange1.png")

Step 2. Fill in all required fields including Current Password, New Password, and Verify Password.

Step 3. Click on "Change Password".
