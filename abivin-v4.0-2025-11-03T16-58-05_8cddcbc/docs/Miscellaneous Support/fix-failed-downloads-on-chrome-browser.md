During the download of the file, please check that the browser has allowed downloading multiple files\
Step 1: Click the download icon as the below picture\
Step 2: Click the Allow sites to download multiple files

![1299](https://files.readme.io/19b183f-9c51c46-11.png "9c51c46-11.png")

You also change setting your browser:\
Step 1: Open Google Chrome and go to Settings.\
Step 2: Scroll all the way down and click on "Show advanced settings".\
Step 3: Go to "Privacy" > "Content Settings".\
In the "Automatic Downloads" section, select "Allow all sites to download multiple files automatically"
