TeamViewer QuickSupport is a software that can connect your computer to your Android, iPhone and iPad phones with ease.

## How to install TeamViewer QuickSupport for iOS

Step 1:  Download the TeamViewer QuickSupport app to your phone. Link download: [https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/teamviewer-quicksupport/id661649585](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/teamviewer-quicksupport/id661649585)\
Step 2: Then open TeamViewer QuickSupport and get your ID number.

<Image title="JVpY1WvXTsqyJuo5F490Gg.png" alt={708} width="100%" src="https://files.readme.io/2ff5093-JVpY1WvXTsqyJuo5F490Gg.png">
  Image 1: Get your ID number
</Image>

Step 3: When there is a connection notification from another phone on your phone, press Allow. TeamViewer QuickSupport will immediately be connected to the partner computer

<Image title="ifa4HaEaTXaSBG47xVC7kg.png" alt={294} width="smart" src="https://files.readme.io/5dcf1e4-ifa4HaEaTXaSBG47xVC7kg.png">
  Image 2: Allow connect to the partner computer
</Image>
