## Receiving

Receiving is the process of confirming that you received what you order. Data collected from receiving determines what your accounting deparment pays for your [suppliers](http://google.com).

## Put away

Once the goods are confirmed in full. A "Put away" process will be triggered. This happens from the Warehouse Manager to bring items from the truck container or the unloading area to the warehouse shelves.
